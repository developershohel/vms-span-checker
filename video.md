## Video Production Tips

### Color Palette
- Primary: #0073aa (WordPress Blue)
- Secondary: #1e3a5f (Dark Blue)
- Accent: #00a0d2 (Light Blue)
- Success: #46b450 (Green)
- Danger: #dc3232 (Red)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter)
- Body: Clean, readable (Open Sans, Roboto)

### Music Resources
- Epidemic Sound
- Artlist
- YouTube Audio Library (free)

### AI Video Tools
- Synthesia (AI avatars)
- Runway ML (AI video generation)
- Pictory (script to video)
- InVideo (templates)
- Canva Video (simple edits)
- HeyGen (AI spokesperson)

### Recommended Resolutions
| Platform | Resolution | Aspect |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| YouTube | 1920x1080 | 16:9 |
| Instagram Feed | 1080x1080 | 1:1 |
| Instagram Story/Reels | 1080x1920 | 9:16 |
| TikTok | 1080x1920 | 9:16 |
| Facebook | 1280x720 | 16:9 |
| Twitter | 1280x720 | 16:9 |
